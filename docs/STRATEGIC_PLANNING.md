# LTTA Strategic Planning (2025-2026)

This document tracks fundamental philosophical and structural questions for the LTTA Leadership.

---

## Core Philosophical Questions
1.  **Definition of Success:** What does competitive balance look like in our league, and how do we measure it?
2.  **The "Social vs. Skill" Tension:** How do we handle the conflict between players choosing to play with friends/partners versus the need for fair competition?
3.  **Governance & Authority:** How do we divide responsibility between league organizers and team captains regarding roster management?
4.  **Process Definition:** What should the lifecycle look like from registration to final roster approval?

## Key Stances & Goals (Brett's Philosophy)
*   **"Fun but Competitive"** over "Win at all costs."
*   **Active Intervention:** We should not just leave it to teams to build the "best" roster; we need clear standards to prevent level-stagnation.

---

## Case Study: "No Openings" Dilemma
*Scenario: If a strong #2 on Team A needs to move up but there are no #1 slots available on any team, what is the protocol?*
*   **Current Issue:** Only having suggestions (not rules) makes it ineffective.
*   **Proposed Fixes:** Swapping (Relegation/Promotion) to ensure an opening always exists when earned.
*   **Roster Risk:** Potential to break up teams or remove a captain.
