## Proposal: Roster Renumbering and Player Movement
**Source:** Lewis Kuhlman (507-381-7749)
**Date Received:** March 2025

### 1. Differentiating Doubles Lines
*   **Suggestion:** With the move to four lines of doubles, Lewis suggests moving away from the "three #3s" terminology which can be confusing.
*   **Proposed Options:**
    *   Number doubles teams sequentially (Line 1, Line 2, Line 3, Line 4).
    *   Rank players individually 1 through 8.
*   **Goal:** Clearer differentiation between the two intermediate (#3) doubles teams.

### 2. Promotion and Relegation System
*   **Suggestion:** Implement a system where the top player/team at each skill level is promoted and the bottom player/team is relegated.
*   **Edge Case Handling:**
    *   Top #1s who can't go higher and bottom #4s/5s who can't go lower could be encouraged to move to the sub list.
*   **Goal:** Increase match variety and ensure competitive balance by moving players based on performance rather than just self-rating.

## Internal Committee Notes
*   *Note (March 2025):* These suggestions align with the committee's goal of "Variety and more competitive matches" mentioned in the preseason meeting.
*   *Technical Note:* Moving to an 8-player "Line 1-4" system would simplify the database schema for the new 4th line of doubles.
