# LTTA League Information & Reference

This document serves as the central reference for the La Crosse Team Tennis Association (LTTA), covering committee roles, league rules, and organizational responsibilities.

---

## 2026 LTTA Committee: Roles and Responsibilities

### League Coordinator – Brett Meddaugh
*   **Communication:** Handles league announcements regarding format, rules, schedules, standings, and updates.
*   **Schedule Management:** Maintains and updates the league schedule.
*   **Scoring & Standings:** Maintains and updates scoring results for the season and provides periodic updates of team standings.
*   **Budget:** Maintains and updates the league budget.
*   **Events:** Works with captains to organize league playoffs and the season picnic.
*   **Meetings:** Organizes and facilitates the annual captains’ meeting and provides the league summary and budget.
*   **City Liaison:** Coordinates with the City’s Park and Rec on annual court reservations.

### Teams Coordinator – Jennifer Carr
*   **Recruitment:** Handles recruitment of players and team captains.
*   **Rosters:** Works with captains to confirm team rosters and fill open positions.
*   **Placement:** Advises on the placement of players and evaluates placement at the end of the season based on match records (optional).
*   **Data Management:** Maintains and updates team rosters, the substitution players’ list, and the contact information database for all players.
*   **Incidents:** Maintains player and team incident reports.

### On-Site Coordinators
**Tuesday Night:** Tom Dwyer | **Wednesday Night:** Mark/Nicole Hoff
*   **Fees:** Collects league fees.
*   **Scoring:** Submits scoring sheets to the League Coordinator.
*   **Weather:** Cancels league play due to rain or heat by notifying team captains (who then inform their players).
*   **Match Oversight:** Ensures matches start on time, helps direct players to assigned courts, and tries to fill spots for no-shows.

## League Rules & Guidelines (Summer 2026)

*Detailed rules are maintained in `public/rules_context.md`. Below is a summary of key points.*

### Overview
*   **Season Window:** June 3 – August 14 (Tuesday & Wednesday nights).
*   **Match Site:** Green Island Park (13 outdoor courts).
*   **Player Fee:** $25 per roster slot (covers balls and courts).

### Match Play Guidelines
*   **Start Times:** 5:30 PM and 7:00 PM.
*   **Warm-ups:** Limited to 10 minutes.
*   **Forfeits:** Take effect at 5:45 PM and 7:15 PM.
*   **Scoring Format:**
    *   No-Ad Scoring (15, 30, 40, Game; Deuce = deciding point).
    *   Best 2 of 3 sets.
    *   Set Tiebreak (at 6-6): 7-point tiebreak.
    *   3rd Set: 7-point tiebreak.
*   **Extreme Heat Rule:**
    *   If "Feels Like" > 95°F: Sets can start at 2-2 by request.
    *   If "Feels Like" > 104°F: Play is cancelled by 4:30 PM.

### Team Organization & Skill Levels
Each team roster (7 players) follows this structure:
*   **#1:** Advanced (1 player)
*   **#2:** High Intermediate (1 player)
*   **#3:** Intermediate (3 players)
*   **#4:** Low Intermediate (1 player)
*   **#5:** Novice (1 player)

**Doubles Pairings:** #1/#2, #3/#3, #4/#5.

### Scoring & Standings
*   Standings are based on **Winning Percentage** (Total Points Earned ÷ Total Points Available).
*   Points per match vary based on result (e.g., 10 for a win, 8 for a split-set loss, etc.).
*   Tiebreaker: Head-to-Head record.
*   Championship: Tuesday winner vs. Wednesday winner.
