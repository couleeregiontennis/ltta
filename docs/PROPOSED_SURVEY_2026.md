# Proposed 2026 LTTA Member Survey

This survey is designed to gauge member sentiment on league balance and organizational authority, as suggested by Lewis Kuhlman.

---

## Part 1: The "Fun vs. Competitive" Balance
*Scale: 1 (Purely Fun) to 5 (Highly Competitive)*

1.  **Personal Preference:** Thinking of last season, what was the right balance of competition and fun for you?
2.  **Match Quality:** Where would you rank the competitiveness of your specific matches?
3.  **Team Dynamic:** Where would you rank the focus of your team?
4.  **League Overall:** Where would you rank the league's current balance?

## Part 2: Organizational Authority
*Scale: 1 (Individuals/Captains) to 5 (League Organizers)*

1.  **Moving Up:** Who should have a greater role in deciding whether a player should move up?
2.  **Moving Down:** Who should have a greater role in deciding whether a player should move down?
3.  **Partnerships:** Who should have a greater role in deciding whether a player should switch partners?
4.  **Team Placement:** Who should have a greater role in deciding whether a player should switch teams?

---

## Part 3: Open Feedback
*   Are you in favor of a performance-based promotion/relegation system (e.g., swapping top Line 3 players with bottom Line 2 players)?
*   How should we handle situations where a player is too strong for their level but there are no openings at the next level up?
