# LTTA Season Picnic Logistics & Feedback

This document tracks planning, feedback, and logistics for the annual LTTA season picnic.

---

## 2024 Picnic Feedback
**Source:** Jim (Captain/Player)
**Date:** Post-2024 Season

### Issues Encountered
1.  **Food Shortages:** Late-finishing players (specifically those in the championship match) arrived at the food tent to find all pizza gone and only one sub remaining.
2.  **Hoarding:** Reports of individuals taking entire plates of pizza or multiple cake slices to their vehicles during the matches.
3.  **Attendance:** Potential that more than the expected 75 people attended.

### Proposed Solutions for 2026
*   **Supervision:** Assign a volunteer to the food table to monitor portions and deter hoarding.
*   **Championship Reservation:** Order additional pizzas and subs specifically to be set aside and "stashed" for the players competing in the championship match.
*   **Order Increase:** Increase the base order of pizzas and subs to accommodate a slightly larger crowd.
